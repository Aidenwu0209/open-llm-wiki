# Attention Is All You Need

## One-Sentence Conclusion

The paper introduced the Transformer, a sequence transduction architecture based
on multi-head self-attention that reached 27.3 BLEU on WMT 2014 English-German
with the base model and 28.4 BLEU with the big model.

## Why It Matters

Recurrent and convolutional models process sequence structure indirectly through
time steps or local windows. The Transformer replaces that path with
self-attention, allowing each token to attend directly to other positions in the
sequence. Positional encodings inject order information that attention alone
does not provide.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced the Transformer architecture based entirely on attention mechanisms
- Reached state-of-the-art on WMT 2014 English-to-German translation
- Demonstrated that self-attention can replace recurrence and convolution as the primary sequence model

## Methods & Data

The Transformer uses stacked self-attention and point-wise, fully connected
layers for both encoder and decoder. The base model has 6 encoder and 6 decoder
layers, 8 attention heads, and approximately 65M parameters. Training used the
WMT 2014 English-German dataset.
