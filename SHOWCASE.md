## What a Query Looks Like

**User asks**: "How did DeepSeek's architecture evolve from V2 to V4?"

**Agent does**:
1. Searches 8 relevant source pages + 4 concept pages
2. Synthesizes the innovation timeline above
3. Writes comparison table back to `deepseek-methodology` concept page
4. Logs: `query-writeback | deepseek-methodology | "V2→V4 evolution"`

**Result**: The next person who asks this question gets the answer instantly —and it's already in the wiki, growing with every new paper.
